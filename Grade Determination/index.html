Grade Determination

Based on your entered marks, the program will determine your grade:
  • If your marks are 90 or above, you will receive Grade A.
  • If your marks are between 80 and 89, you will receive Grade B.
  • If your marks are between 70 and 79, you will receive Grade C
  • If your marks are between 60 and 69, you will receive Grade D.
  • If your marks are below 60, you will receive Grade F
